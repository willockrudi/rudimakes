Bench Fee: A $45 diagnostic fee is charged on all jobs. This fee is applied toward your repair total if you approve the work. If you decline the repair after diagnosis, the $45 bench fee is non-refundable.

Estimates: You will receive a written estimate before any repair work begins. No work proceeds without your approval unless the total falls under the threshold you specified.

Warranty: All labor carries a 30-day warranty from the date of completion. Parts warranties vary by manufacturer.

Storage: Equipment not picked up within 30 days of completion will accrue a $5/day storage fee. Equipment unclaimed after 90 days may be considered abandoned.

Liability: Filament Audio is not responsible for pre-existing damage or failures unrelated to the work performed.
