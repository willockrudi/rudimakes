Architecture

How It Was Built

ARIA is a RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) system trained on over 8,000 Roland-specific sources — product manuals, support documentation, forum archives, YouTube transcripts, and 67 hand-written technical reference documents covering every Roland product ever made. The knowledge base was built by a working backline technician who understands what questions actually get asked in professional production environments.
